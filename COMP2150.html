COMP2150 Game Design

A summary...

Here is a summary of a ‘level design’ assignment I completed for this unit. I have to design three level of a game. The tools to do so are provided, I just need to draw a map or level plan for each level and make a story board for all the mechanic of the game. In the first level all the mechanic of the game must be introduced, and the level need to teach the player how to play the game. In the later level their difficulty needs to increase in a reasonable rate while allowing the player to practice and use the skills they just learnt in previous level(s).
